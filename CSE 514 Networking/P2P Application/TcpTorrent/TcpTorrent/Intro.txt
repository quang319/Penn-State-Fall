Hello! Welcome to TCP P2P file sharing program!
Below are the commands that you may use to operate the program:

	- ListUploads	--> Will provide you with a list of all the files from your desktop that you can share to your peers
					(Note: You may only upload files from your desktop)

	- Upload-#		--> Will upload the index of the file that was shown with the "ListUploads" command

	- ListDownloads --> will provide you with a list of all the files that are available for you to download from your peers

	- Download-#	--> Will download the index of the file that was shown with the "ListDownloads"

	- Help			--> Will present you with this prompt again
